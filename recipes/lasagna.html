Lasagna

Description

This is a quick and easy lasagna recipe that should easily feed a family on a budget

Ingrediants

  • 1 16oz package of lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound of lean ground beef
  • 1 16oz jar of spaghetti sauce
  • 1 clove of garlic, minced
  • 1/2 pound of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 pound of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 pint of ricotta cheese
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil
  2. Add pasta and cook for 8-10 minutes and then drain
  3. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  4. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, brown beef and season with salt and pepper and then drain
  5. Stir in spaghetti sauce and garlic and simmer 5 minutes
  6. In a medium bowl, combine mozzarella, cheddar and ricotta and stir well
  7. In 9x13 inch pan, alternate layers of noodles, meat mixture and cheese mixture until pan is filled
  8. Back in preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly
